Why

Driving forces

shushjax was designed to deliver the benefits of AJAX-style page loading, free from complicated frameworks. Compared to the original PJAX, shushjax offers additional flexibility, performance, and ease of implementation. No server header or special backend requirements means that shushjax can be used on static hosts and CDNs like GitHub pages and S3. No jQuery required means that the browser does not need to load and process tens of kilobytes of unused javascript, increasing performance
